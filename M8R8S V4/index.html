M8R8S Studio is a London based photography and digital studio that specialises in the documentation of visual art through photography and digital reproductions.

We operate with an in-depth understanding of contemporary art and place particular attention to accurate colour representation inline with cultural heritage standards in photography and digital reproduction practices.

Our services span photography, digital retouching, and 3D modelling.

Get in touch for availability or if you have any questions about our services. We're always happy to chat and come up with solutions that might suit your project.
